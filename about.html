About Us

Born and raised in Jamaica you not only enjoy beautiful beaches, you get a chance to explore a variety of foods. After making the move to Chicago, we quickly realized there was a lack of authentic Caribbean food. That is when our journey began.

Enjoy

With different levels in our venue you can chose the perfect spot to enjoy your dinner. Sit in the main dinning room, or boast at the beautiful skyline on our rooftop lounge.

Entertain

Keeping it as authentic as possible, we bring artist and DJs directly from Jamaica. Dancing the night away is an elevator ride away.
